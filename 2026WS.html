総合まとめ Overall Summary

全体の満足度Overall Satisfaction 両日とも「とても良かった」が 50%。2日目は「期待と違った」の回答がゼロで改善。両日合計で「まぁまぁ良かった」以上が 84.8%。 "Very Good" at 50% on both days. Day 2 had zero "Below Expectations" responses, showing improvement. Combined "Good" or above: 84.8%.
講師評価の変化Instructor Rating Trend 2日目は「とても良かった」が 71.4%（1日目は42.1%）と大幅に向上。「普通」の回答も2日目はゼロに。 Day 2 "Very Good" jumped to 71.4% (vs. 42.1% on Day 1). "Average" responses dropped to zero on Day 2.
参加動機Participation Motives 「ヘザーさんから直接学びたい」「スキルアップ」「特定の症状へのアプローチ習得」が三大動機。 Top 3 motives: "Learn directly from Heather," "Skill development," and "Learn approaches for specific conditions."
改善要望Improvement Requests 1日目は「もう少し深い内容」「疾患別の具体的対応」への期待あり。2日目もロングコース・ケーススタディの要望。 Day 1: desire for deeper, condition-specific content. Day 2: requests for longer courses and case studies.
人気テーマPopular Future Topics 「各種器具（チェア・バレル等）」と「クラシカルの深掘り」が最多。「解剖学×実技」「リハビリ」「ケーススタディ」も需要高。 "Equipment (Chair, Barrel, etc.)" and "Classical Pilates deep-dive" most requested. "Anatomy × Practice," "Rehab," and "Case Studies" also in high demand.
集客チャネルAcquisition Channels PSGA公式LINEが約57%で最大。Instagram/SNSが40%で続き、SNSの強化で更なる集客が期待できる。 PSGA Official LINE leads at ~57%. Instagram/SNS follows at 40% — strengthening social media could further boost sign-ups.
